About

About Modulus:

Welcome to Modulus Tutors! We are a leading provider of educational support, offering a wide range of tutoring services to students of all ages and levels. Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced tutors who are dedicated to helping students succeed academically.
Whether you need help with a specific subject, preparing for exams, or improving your study skills, we are here to help. Our tutors are experts in their fields and are passionate about helping students reach their full potential.
We offer both online and in-person tutoring options to accommodate your needs and schedule. Our online platform provides a convenient way to connect with a tutor from anywhere, while our in-person tutoring offers a more personalized experience.
At our tutoring service, we understand that every student is unique, which is why we tailor our tutoring sessions to meet your individual needs. Whether you are struggling with a particular concept or looking to excel in a specific subject, our tutors will work with you to create a customized learning plan that works for you.
We are committed to providing high-quality tutoring services that are affordable and accessible to all students. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve your academic goals..

How to join us?

  1. Click on the WhatsApp group invitation link.
  2. Join our Modulus tutor group.
  3. An inquiry has been posted in the group. If you are interested, please direct message the administrator.
  4. We will link you up with parents.

How to become a professional teacher?

To become a professional teacher, attend classes regularly for two months and pay your fees on time. Upon completion, you will be moved to the Modulus professional group and receive a Modulus kit from us.
